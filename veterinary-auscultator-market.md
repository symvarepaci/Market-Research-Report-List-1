<p><p>The veterinary auscultator market features two primary types: single head and double head auscultators. Single head auscultators are lightweight and designed for basic examinations, offering ease of use for quick assessments. In contrast, double head auscultators provide enhanced listening capabilities, enabling veterinarians to discern subtle sounds in animal heart and lung function. Both types cater to different veterinary needs, with the choice often depending on the complexity of the examination and the specific requirements of animal healthcare professionals.</p></p>
