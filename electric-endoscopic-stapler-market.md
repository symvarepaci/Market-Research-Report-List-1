<p><p>The Electric Endoscopic Stapler market includes three primary types: linear, standard, and circular staplers. Linear staplers are designed for cutting and stapling tissue in a straight line, making them suitable for procedures like gastrointestinal surgeries. Standard staplers typically refer to more general-use devices for various surgical applications. Circular staplers create a circular line of staples, ideal for anastomosis in procedures such as colorectal surgeries. Each type serves specific surgical functions, enhancing efficiency and outcomes in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
